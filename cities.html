col-xs : For Extra small devices(screen width less than 576 px )
col-sm :For small devices(screen width greater than or equal to 576 px)
col-md : For medium devices(screen width greater than or equal to 768 px)
col-lg : For large devices(screen width greater than or equal to 992 px)
